What Is Covid-19

Coronavirus

Corona viruses are a type of virus. There are many different kinds, and some cause disease. A newly identified type has caused a recent outbreak of respiratory illness now called COVID-19. This "novel" corona virus seems to be more infectious than others which is why it is more dangerous.

Covid-19

Contagion

As mentioned before, this strain of corona virus is more infectious and spreads in the following ways:

Air-Transmission

Covid 19 is transmissible through droplets produced when an infected individual coughs or sneezes. It is thus, Airborne.

Human Contacts

Due to our habits of contact, Covid can be transmitted through touch with an infected individual.

Contained Objects

Covid can survive on most objects if touched by an infected individual. Thus it can spread indirectly.
